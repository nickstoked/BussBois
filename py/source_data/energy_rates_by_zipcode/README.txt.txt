U.S. Electric Utility Companies and Rates: Look-up by Zipcode (2020)
This data represents the residential, commercial, and industrial energy rates in $/kWh by zipcode and state info. The two data sets represent Ivenster Owned Utitlies (IOUs) as well as non-IOUs. 


Data collected at this link: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/u-s-electric-utility-companies-and-rates-look-up-by-zipcode-2020
